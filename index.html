Our Facilities

Library

The Borchardt library at La Trobe University is a comprehensive resource for students and faculty. It houses an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources as well as world class staff to support academic research and learning. The library also provides comfortable study spaces and access to specialized databases.

Sports Center

The La Trobe Indoor sports center offers state-of-the-art facilities for various sports and fitness activities. It includes a gym, swimming pool, and various indoor sports halls. The center also hosts fitness classes, consultations, and organized sports events throughout the year.

Labs

La Trobe University boasts state-of-the-art and well-equipped science labs that promotes hands-on learning and experimentation. Equipped with advanced instruments and technologies, they provide students with a conducive environment for continuous scientific exploration and research.
