Uncovering the Beth Gleeson Building: Where Innovation Meets Fun at La Trobe!

Beth Glee...son?

That's right! Ever wondered where the magic happens at La Trobe University? Look no further than the Beth Gleeson Building! Named after the incredible former Vice-Chancellor, this place is where the cool kids come together for some serious fun and learning. Let's dive into what makes the building a must-see spot on campus and our conference location for the AGM!
